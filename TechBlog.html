Margins vs Padding

Margins:
  • Outside of block elements
  • Use margin to separate the block from things outside of it


Padding:
  • Clears an area around the content of an element.
  • Affected by the background color of the element.
  • The top, right, bottom, and left padding can be changed independently using separate properties.
    • A shorthand padding property can also be used, to change all paddings at once.
  • Borders allow you to specify the style and color of an element's border.
